Just another random thing that popped into my head. I thought of it as a kind of "Austin Powers" esque lounge/jazz/psych theme song for a fictional action hero (that has no name and no description). Then, like I do with many of my Suno songs, I reimagined it as breakbeat. Because always breakbeat.

I think this is another example of Suno just totally taking some random snippet of music of mine directly out of my head and making something real out of it. It's just fun.
