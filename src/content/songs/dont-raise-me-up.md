I was actually playing my *actual acoustic guitar* and I found a cool chord riff that I liked. I quickly recorded it and uploaded it to Suno to see what I could do with it.

**This is not that sample.**

The original sample got flagged as copyright encumbered. When I asked Claude what song it could possibly be (I couldn't recall myself), it suggested that it was just too generic of a sample, and that Suno was being conservative. This makes sense, I've heard of Suno rejecting people's lyrics because they were like "I love you like the stars in the sky". Sorry, someone has used that already!

I recorded a different chord progression and then wrote these lyrics. I think it came out pretty good, but I was hoping for a more MGMT/Tame Impala vibe, and this sounds too much like Creed.
