This is the perfect application of Suno, honestly. I just wrote some quick lyrics to illustrate the point of a new website/pamphlet I made, [https://nevergivethemyourface.com](https://nevergivethemyourface.com). *[Side note: I wanted that site to be anonymous but I guess I'm violating that here. I'm sure someone would have been able to figure it out]*

I knew the feel of the song I wanted, Dropkick Murphys/heartland punk. So I just wrote it up and typed it into Suno.

The point is the lyrics, the artistic statement, not the recording or the particular expression. It would *not* have made sense for me to spend 5-8 hours recording and mixing this, for a 90 second song that 8 people are going to listen to.
