This is it. I took the full mastered version of my song *You're A Star,* the version that's you know *on Spotify and [YouTube Music](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O2pgZ_qRJMI)* (where it has a beautiful 37 views), and plugged it into fucking Suno. I watched as my creative life of the past 20+ years melted into a puddle before my eyes. I knew it would come to this, I knew this was the final test, the final boss as it were. I actually did this a few days ago and resisted posting it.

In a sense, this version is just as much a demo as my version. Does the Suno version sound better? I would say it just sounds more polished in a way, maybe more exaggerated? More full? I don't know. I don't know anything. I certainly don't know what to do.
