This song was the end result of hours and hours of arranging and mixing. It still counts as a Suno experiment, and I'm not claiming otherwsise. But it is a hybrid, I'm also claiming it as a song I *wrote*. I am posting it on [my original demos website](https://songs.travisbriggs.com).

The methodology started with humming a riff again. I tried really hard to get Suno to do "Only Guitar". I had an idea that I could hum all the parts individually and then mash them together in Ableton. That basically didn't work at all. Suno would always produce bass and drums. So I adjusted course.

**I extracted "Stems" from the 50 second song it produced and got a "guitar stem". Though it wasn't a crisp guitar stem, there was all kinds of bleed and effects and layering. I think the idea that Suno can produce stems gets people excited, but isn't always particularly useful.**

Anyways, realizing how messy this sample was, I thought...."chop it up!". I just resequenced it heavily and wrote a new song around it.

So in the final analysis, Suno worked as kind of a synth, where I hummed something and got a guitar sample.
