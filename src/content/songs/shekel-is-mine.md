Once again, it is a Saturday morning and I am singing a song that popped into my head (er, I mean....I wrote) to my cat.

As usual, I sing it lazily into my phone and send to my wife and her sister. But this time! I decided to take the sample and turn it into a Suno song, for max camp and comedic effect.

And before I even did that, I made an exercise out of it. I brought the original sample into Live and added a verse, plus some vocal harmonies. Still just really a layered vocal sketch and I didn't put much effort into getting this on time or even in tune.

Finally I decided to completely nuts and recorded a few more layers for Sample+. That one is kind of unhinged and I feel a little bit embarassed posting it. I already had the target genre in mind when I added that stuff.
